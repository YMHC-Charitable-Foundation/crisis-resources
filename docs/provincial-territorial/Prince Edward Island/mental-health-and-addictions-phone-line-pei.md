title: "Mental Health and Addictions Phone Line (PEI)"
---

## About
A **toll-free, 24/7** phone line for mental health and addictions support in Prince Edward Island. Calls are answered by trained mental health professionals (a registered nurse or a social worker).

## Coverage
**Prince Edward Island (Canada).**

## Who it’s for
- Anyone in PEI who needs **immediate** mental health or addictions support
- People supporting someone else and looking for guidance or next steps

## Contact
- **Call (toll-free):** 1-833-553-6983
- **Website:** https://www.princeedwardisland.ca/en/information/health-pei/mental-health-and-addictions-services

## Hours
- **24/7/365**

## What to expect when you call
- You’ll be connected with a trained mental health professional who can help assess what’s going on, provide support, and connect you to services.
- If in-person support is needed, the **Mobile Mental Health Response Service** can be accessed through this line **from 10 a.m. to 10 p.m. daily**.
