**Location:** Northwest Territories, Canada  
**Resource type:** Registered nurse advice line (includes mental wellness and addictions support)  
**Hours:** 24/7  

## Contact

- **Dial:** 811
- **Toll-free:** 1-844-259-1793
- **Website:** https://www.hss.gov.nt.ca/en/services/811

## About

Confidential advice and support from registered nurses, including mental wellness and addiction recovery guidance (see GNWT site).

## Important

- If you or someone else is in immediate danger, call **911** (or go to the nearest emergency department).
- If you can’t get through, try another local line, or dial **211** where available for service navigation.
