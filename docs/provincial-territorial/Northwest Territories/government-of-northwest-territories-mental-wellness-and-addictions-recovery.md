## About
The Government of the Northwest Territories (GNWT) **Mental Wellness and Addictions Recovery** pages are a central directory of **territory-wide supports**, including **helplines (811)**, **online supports**, **community-based supports**, and **facility-based addictions treatment**.

## Coverage
- **Northwest Territories (Canada)**

## Who it’s for
- NWT residents seeking **mental wellness** or **substance use / addiction recovery** supports
- People supporting someone else who needs help

## Contact
- **Website:** https://www.hss.gov.nt.ca/en/services/mental-wellness-and-addictions-recovery
- **Phone (GNWT HSS):** 867-767-9061
- **Email:** mha@gov.nt.ca

## Notes
- The GNWT site advises: **Call 911 if you need immediate help.**
- For 24/7 nurse support related to mental wellness and addictions recovery, see **811** under **Help Lines**.
