**Location:** Nunavut, Canada  
**Resource type:** Help line (phone)  

## Contact

- **Call (toll-free):** 1-800-265-3333
- **Call (local):** 867-979-3333
- **Website:** https://nunavuthelpline.ca/helpline

## About

Anonymous and confidential support by phone, staffed by trained volunteers (see site).

## Important

- If you or someone else is in immediate danger, call **911** (or go to the nearest emergency department).
- If you can’t get through, try another local line, or dial **211** where available for service navigation.
