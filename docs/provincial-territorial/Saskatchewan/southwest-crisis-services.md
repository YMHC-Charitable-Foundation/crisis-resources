## About

Southwest Crisis Services (SWCS) offers 24‑hour crisis support, safe shelter and counselling for individuals and families experiencing domestic violence or other crises in the southwest corner of Saskatchewan. The organisation is trauma‑ and domestic‑violence‑informed and has provided services since 1984. Programs include emergency shelter, outreach counselling, sexual violence services and community education.

## Coverage

Southwest Saskatchewan – including Swift Current and surrounding rural communities.

## Who it’s for

- Adults and families experiencing domestic or sexual violence.
- People in crisis needing immediate safety, counselling, information or referrals.
- Individuals seeking community education or outreach services in the southwest region.

## Languages

- **English** (other languages may be arranged through interpreters or translation services).

## What to expect when you call

A crisis worker will provide confidential, non‑judgmental support, safety planning and referrals. For those requiring shelter, staff can arrange safe accommodation and basic necessities. Counsellors offer individual and group counselling for adults and children and can travel to communities throughout the southwest.
