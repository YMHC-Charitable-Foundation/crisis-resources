## About

The Hudson Bay Family & Support Centre (HBFSC) provides crisis support, education and programming to individuals and families experiencing violence or other crises in the Hudson Bay area. Their mission is to provide tools that promote healthy, violence‑free living.

## Coverage

Hudson Bay and surrounding district in northeast Saskatchewan.

## Who it’s for

- Individuals and families experiencing domestic violence or other crises.
- Anyone in the Hudson Bay area needing support, education or referrals for personal safety and wellbeing.

## Hours

- **Office hours:** Monday–Friday 9:00 am – 4:00 pm.
- **Crisis line:** Available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

## Languages

- **English** (callers can request interpreter support).

## What to expect when you call

During office hours, staff can provide information, safety planning, outreach and referrals. Outside office hours, the 24‑hour crisis line connects you to a responder who can offer immediate support and connect you to shelter, counselling or other services as needed.
