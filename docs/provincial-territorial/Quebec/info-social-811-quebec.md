**Location:** Quebec, Canada  
**Resource type:** Psychosocial consultation and referral (phone)  
**Hours:** 24/7  

## Contact

- **Call:** 811
- **Website:** https://www.quebec.ca/en/health/finding-a-resource/info-social-811

## About

Telephone consultation with a psychosocial worker and guidance to resources.

## Important

- If you or someone else is in immediate danger, call **911** (or go to the nearest emergency department).
- If you can’t get through, try another local line, or dial **211** where available for service navigation.
