## About
211 New Brunswick is a **free, confidential** information and referral service that helps people find and navigate **community, social, health, and government services**.

## Coverage
- **New Brunswick**

## Who it’s for
- Anyone in New Brunswick who needs help finding supports or services
- Family/friends or service providers looking for referrals and local resources

## Hours
- **24/7**

## Languages
- **150+ languages** (including **English and French**)

## What to expect when you call
A trained 211 navigator will ask what you’re looking for and help connect you to relevant services and next steps. 211 is **confidential**, and you generally do not need to share more personal information than you’re comfortable with.
