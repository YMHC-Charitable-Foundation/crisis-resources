## About
MindWell is an **online mental health and wellness platform** with classes and training resources (for example, mindfulness and well-being learning).

## Coverage
**New Brunswick** (online access)

## Who it’s for
- People in New Brunswick who want **self-guided mental wellness tools** and classes
- Anyone looking to build habits for stress, focus, and resilience

## Contact
- **New Brunswick access page:** https://app.mindwellu.com/newbrunswick/landing
- **MindWell website:** https://www.mindwellu.com/

## Hours
- **Online, on-demand.** (Live sessions/classes vary.)

## Languages
- **English and French**

## What to expect when you sign up
- Create an account on the New Brunswick access page
- Use the platform’s classes/training resources at your own pace (and join live sessions when offered)

## Notes
- MindWell is **not** an emergency service.
