## About
A Friendly Voice is a **free, confidential “warm line”** for **older adults (55+)** focused on **connection and friendly conversation** (not counselling).

## Coverage
- **New Brunswick** (also available in Ontario and other Atlantic provinces)

## Who it’s for
- Older adults (55+) who are feeling lonely, isolated, or just want someone to talk to

## Contact
- **Call (toll-free):** 1-855-892-9992
- **Email:** info@afriendlyvoice.ca
- **Website:** https://afriendlyvoice.ca/

## Hours
- **7 days/week, 8 a.m.–10 p.m.**

## Languages
- Service language is not clearly stated on the program’s main page; **ask when you call**.

## What to expect when you call
You’ll be connected with a trained volunteer for a warm, supportive conversation. Volunteers **do not provide advice or counselling** and will help direct crisis situations to emergency/crisis services.

## Notes
- If you’re in immediate danger or need urgent crisis support, call **911** (or your local emergency number).
