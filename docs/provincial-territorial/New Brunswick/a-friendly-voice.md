**Location:** New Brunswick, Canada  
**Resource type:** Peer support phone line (older adults)  
**Hours:** 8:00 a.m.–10:00 p.m., 7 days/week (check site)  

## Contact

- **Call:** 1-855-892-9992
- **Website:** https://afriendlyvoice.ca/

## About

Free and confidential peer support phone line for older adults (55+).

## Important

- If you or someone else is in immediate danger, call **911** (or go to the nearest emergency department).
- If you can’t get through, try another local line, or dial **211** where available for service navigation.
