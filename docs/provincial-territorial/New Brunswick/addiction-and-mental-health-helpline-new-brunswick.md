**Location:** New Brunswick, Canada  
**Resource type:** Provincial helpline  
**Hours:** 24/7  

## Contact

- **Call:** 1-866-355-5550
- **Website:** https://www2.gnb.ca/content/gnb/en/departments/health/AddictionsandMentalHealth/helpline.html

## About

Information, support and connections to services for New Brunswick residents (all ages).

## Important

- If you or someone else is in immediate danger, call **911** (or go to the nearest emergency department).
- If you can’t get through, try another local line, or dial **211** where available for service navigation.
