## About
The New Brunswick Addiction and Mental Health Helpline is a **confidential, toll-free, bilingual** line for people of **all ages** who want to talk about concerns related to **mental health, substance use/addiction, or gambling harms**.

## Coverage
- **New Brunswick**

## Who it’s for
- Anyone in New Brunswick seeking support for mental health, addiction/substance use, or gambling harms
- Family/friends/caregivers or community agencies seeking guidance and referrals

## Contact
- **Call (toll-free):** 1-866-355-5550

## Hours
- **24/7 (round-the-clock)**
- The helpline page notes trained clinicians are available **8 a.m.–midnight**, with **Tele-Care 811 staff** providing overnight support as part of a connected system.

## Languages
- **English and French (bilingual)**

## What to expect when you call
Support can include:
- Listening and guidance for mental health, substance use/addiction, and gambling concerns  
- Help for family/friends and community agencies (education and consultation)  
- Navigation and referrals to government and community services  
- Coaching to connect you to information and self-guided supports  
- Crisis intervention and coordination with mobile crisis services or emergency services when appropriate  
- Follow-up phone calls when appropriate  

## Privacy and safety approach
- Described as **confidential**. If there is an immediate safety concern, staff may help connect you to urgent or emergency supports.
