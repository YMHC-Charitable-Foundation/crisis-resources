## About
Strongest Families Institute (SFI) provides **free, skills-based mental health programs** delivered at a distance (phone/web). SFI notes it is **not a counselling service**.

## Coverage
**New Brunswick** (program availability varies by province/territory)

## Who it’s for
In New Brunswick, SFI lists programs for:
- **Parents/caregivers** (e.g., parenting/behaviour support)
- **Children and youth** with mild-to-moderate anxiety and related concerns (age ranges depend on the program)

- **Self-referral / sign-up:** https://login.strongestfamilies.com/signup/sfi-self/

## Hours
- Coaching appointments are arranged with participants (SFI notes they work to accommodate family schedules).

## Languages
- **English and French**

## What to expect when you sign up
- Complete a referral/self-referral request (online or by phone)
- You may be asked to complete a questionnaire and consent process
- You are matched with a coach; program materials may be provided online and/or mailed

## Privacy and safety approach
- SFI states they collect personal information with consent and provide a privacy policy (including how to contact their privacy officer).

## Notes
- SFI is **not** a crisis service.
