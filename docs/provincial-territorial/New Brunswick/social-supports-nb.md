## About
Social Supports NB is a **Government of New Brunswick website** to help people find information about social programs and services in New Brunswick (including supports for seniors, families, and caregivers).

## Coverage
**New Brunswick (province-wide)**

## Who it’s for
- People looking for information on **government and community supports**
- Seniors, families, and caregivers (and anyone helping them navigate services)

## Contact
- **Website:** https://socialsupportsnb.ca/en
- **Help by phone (referrals and navigation):** **2-1-1** (or **1-855-258-4126** toll-free)

## Hours
- Website: **24/7**
- 2-1-1 phone line: **24/7**

## Languages
- **English and French** (2-1-1 can support many other languages)

## Notes
- Social Supports NB is a **navigation tool**, not an emergency service.
