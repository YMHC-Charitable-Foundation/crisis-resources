## About
BounceBack® is a **free**, evidence-based **CBT (cognitive behavioural therapy)** skill-building program run by the **Canadian Mental Health Association of New Brunswick (CMHA NB)**. It offers **telephone coaching** and self-guided materials (workbooks, activities, videos).

## Coverage
- **New Brunswick** (delivered by phone/online; no travel required)

## Who it’s for
- **Youth 15–18** and **adults 19+** who want support for **low mood, mild-to-moderate depression, anxiety, stress, or worry**
- Not meant for emergencies or immediate crisis situations

## Hours
- **By appointment** (telephone sessions are scheduled with a coach)

## Languages
- **Coaching + workbooks:** English and French  
- **Videos:** available in multiple languages (see program site)

## What to expect
- Coaching is delivered in **up to six telephone sessions** (often 15–20 minutes) over **about 3–6 months**
- You can be referred by a primary care provider, or **self-refer** online (the program may contact your primary care provider)
- Workbooks may be mailed to you (you can choose where they are sent)

## Privacy and safety approach
- The program emphasizes privacy and confidentiality, and may complete routine risk checks during coaching sessions. If you need urgent help, use local crisis supports (e.g., **988**, **911**).
