## About
Government of New Brunswick information and entry point for **public mental health and addiction services**, including self-guided supports and how to find care in your area.

## Coverage
**New Brunswick (province-wide)**

## Who it’s for
- People in New Brunswick looking for mental health and/or addiction services
- Families, caregivers, and anyone supporting someone else

## Contact
- **Website:** https://www.gnb.ca/en/topic/health-wellness/mental-health.html
- **24/7 Addiction and Mental Health Helpline (NB):** 1-866-355-5550  
- **Emergency:** 911  
- **Canada 9-8-8 (call/text):** 9-8-8

## Hours
- **Addiction and Mental Health Helpline:** 24/7

## Languages
- **English and French** (Government of New Brunswick services are bilingual)

## What to expect when you call or text
- The **NB Addiction and Mental Health Helpline** can provide support, information, and help connecting to services, including community addiction and mental health services.

## Notes
- This page is an **information and navigation hub**; it isn’t a single crisis line on its own (use the helpline or emergency options above if you need immediate help).
