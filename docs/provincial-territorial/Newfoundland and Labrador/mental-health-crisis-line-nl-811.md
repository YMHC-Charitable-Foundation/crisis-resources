## About
**811 (NL HealthLine)** provides immediate mental health and addictions crisis support in Newfoundland and Labrador and can connect you to local services.

## Coverage
**Newfoundland and Labrador (province-wide).**

## Who it’s for
- Anyone in Newfoundland and Labrador who is in a mental health and/or addictions crisis, needs urgent support, or is unsure where to turn (including if you’re thinking about suicide).

## Contact
- **Call:** **811**
- **TTY:** **1-888-709-3555**
- **VoIP (internet phone):** **1-888-709-2929**

## Hours
- **24/7/365**

## What to expect when you call
- You’ll speak with a trained health professional (registered nurse, and in some cases a nurse practitioner) who can listen, provide crisis support, and help connect you to services in your area.
- If you are in immediate danger, call **911**.

## Notes
If you are thinking about suicide or worried about someone else **anywhere in Canada**, you can also **call or text 9-8-8** (24/7).
