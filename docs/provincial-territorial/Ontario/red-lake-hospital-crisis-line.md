## About
Red Lake Margaret Cochenour Memorial Hospital’s Community Counselling and Addiction Services provides **free and confidential** mental health and addictions supports, including **Mobile Crisis Response**.

## Coverage
- **Ontario:** Red Lake and Ear Falls

## Who it’s for
- People of **all ages** in Red Lake and Ear Falls experiencing a mental health or addictions crisis
- Family/friends/caregivers seeking help in a crisis

## Contact
- **24/7 crisis support line:** 1-866-888-8988
- **Appointments / non-urgent:** 807-727-1100
- **Hospital (main):** 807-727-2231

## Hours
- **Crisis support line:** **24 hours/day**
- **Office (appointments):** Monday–Friday, **8:00 a.m.–4:00 p.m.**

## Languages
Not listed on the official program page (ask when you connect).

## What to expect when you call
- **24/7 phone crisis support** is available.
- **Mobile Crisis Response** is available for crisis situations:
  - Monday–Friday, **10:00 a.m.–2:00 a.m.**
  - **24 hours on weekends**

## Privacy and safety approach
- Services are described as **confidential** by the program.
