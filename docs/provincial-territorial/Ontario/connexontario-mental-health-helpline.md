**Location:** Ontario, Canada  
**Resource type:** Mental health and addictions information & referral  

## Contact

- **Call:** 1-866-531-2600
- **TTY:** 1-866-866-9976
- **Website:** https://www.connexontario.ca/

## About

Free and confidential health services information for people experiencing problems with mental health, addiction, and gambling.

## Important

- If you or someone else is in immediate danger, call **911** (or go to the nearest emergency department).
- If you can’t get through, try another local line, or dial **211** where available for service navigation.
