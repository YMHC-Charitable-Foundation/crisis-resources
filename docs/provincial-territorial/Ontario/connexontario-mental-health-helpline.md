## About
ConnexOntario is a free, confidential service that helps people in Ontario find **mental health, addiction, and problem gambling** services. Support is available by phone, text, live chat, and email.

## Coverage
**Ontario-wide.**

## Who it’s for
- Anyone in Ontario looking for information or help connecting to services
- People seeking help for themselves or someone else

## Contact
- **Call:** 1-866-531-2600
- **Text:** Text **CONNEX** to **247247**
- **Live chat:** Available on ConnexOntario’s website
- **Email:** Use the “Email Us” form on ConnexOntario’s website

## Hours
- **24/7/365**

## Languages
- Phone support is available in **multiple languages** (ConnexOntario reports **130+ languages** supported in the prior year).

## What to expect when you call or text
A live agent will listen, ask a few questions (like your city/postal code) to locate options near you, and share information and next steps. ConnexOntario provides brief supportive listening and information/referrals (not ongoing counselling).

## Privacy and safety approach
ConnexOntario describes the service as confidential, collecting limited personal information as needed. Calls may be recorded for quality/training. If there is a serious risk of harm, they may take steps to contact emergency services.
