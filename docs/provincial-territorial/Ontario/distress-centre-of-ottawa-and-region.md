## About
The Distress Centre of Ottawa and Region operates a **24/7 Distress Line** (emotional support) and a **24/7 Crisis Line** (serious mental health crisis support with connection to crisis teams).

## Coverage
Primarily **Ottawa and the Champlain region (Ontario)**.

## Who it’s for
### Distress Line
- Anyone in distress who needs emotional support (**all ages**)

### Crisis Line
- People experiencing a serious mental health crisis (**16+**), as well as family or friends calling with concern

- **24/7/365**

## Languages
- **Distress Line:** English and French
- **Crisis Line:** English and French

## What to expect when you call or text
### Distress Line
A responder can provide emotional support, crisis intervention, suicide risk support, and referrals to community resources.

### Crisis Line
A responder will assess and support your situation and, if needed (and with consent), connect you to the local Crisis Team.

## Privacy and safety approach
The Crisis Line states that calls are confidential. The Crisis Line and Distress Line are **not emergency services**—if there is an immediate, life-threatening emergency, call **911**.

## Notes
If you are thinking about suicide, you can also call or text **9-8-8** in Canada.
