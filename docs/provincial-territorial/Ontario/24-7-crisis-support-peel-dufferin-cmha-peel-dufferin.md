## About
24.7 Crisis Support Peel Dufferin provides rapid support to help de-escalate and stabilize people experiencing a **mental health or addiction crisis**. Support is available by phone, and may include in-person crisis response depending on location and need.

## Coverage
- **Peel Region**
- **Dufferin County**
- **Phone-only crisis support:** West Woodbridge and North Etobicoke

## Who it’s for
**Peel Region**
- All ages
- Peel Region residents experiencing a mental health or addictions crisis

**Dufferin County**
- Adults **18+**
- Dufferin County residents experiencing a mental health or addictions crisis

## Contact
- **Call:** 905-278-9036
- **Call (toll-free):** 1-888-811-2222
- **Online form (ages 16+):** “24.7 Crisis Alert Form” (see source page)

## Hours
- **24/7/365**

## Notes
- This service is **not an emergency response unit**. If the situation is life-threatening, call **911**.
