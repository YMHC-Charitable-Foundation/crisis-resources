## About
Distress Centre Niagara provides **24/7** crisis and emotional support by phone, plus **text and online chat**.

## Coverage
**Niagara Region (Ontario)** (numbers vary by area).

## Who it’s for
- People in the Niagara Region who are in distress, feeling overwhelmed, or need someone to talk to
- People who prefer **phone**, **text**, or **online chat**

## Hours
- **Phone:** 24/7/365
- **Text and online chat:** 24/7/365

## Languages
Not listed on the website — ask when you connect.

## What to expect when you call or text
- **Text/chat:** You’ll be asked to complete a brief pre-chat survey and agree to the terms and conditions before connecting with a live responder.
- **Phone:** A responder will listen, support you, and help you explore next steps.

## Notes
If you are in immediate danger or need emergency medical help, call **911**.
