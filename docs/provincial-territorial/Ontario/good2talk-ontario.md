## About
Good2Talk is a **free, confidential** mental health support service for **Ontario post-secondary students**, offering professional counselling, volunteer crisis support, and information/referrals.

## Coverage
**Ontario** (for eligible post-secondary students).

## Who it’s for
- Current post-secondary students **residing and studying in Ontario** (including colleges, universities, trade schools, and Indigenous institutions)

## Contact
- **Call:** 1-866-925-5454
- **Text:** Text **GOOD2TALKON** to **686868**
- **Message online:** Use the “Message Good2Talk online” option on the Good2Talk website

- **Website:** https://good2talk.ca/ontario/

## Hours
- **24/7/365**

## Languages
- Phone support is available in **100+ languages** with a professionally trained interpreter.
- You can choose a language when you call.

## What to expect when you call or text
### Calling
After you call, you’ll be welcomed and asked to **select a language**, then follow prompts to continue.

### Texting / messaging online
- You’ll receive a few automated messages about the service and privacy policy.
- Then you’ll be connected with a trained volunteer crisis responder.
- You can end the conversation at any time by texting **STOP**.

## Privacy and safety approach
Good2Talk states your conversation is private and is only shared outside the service if staff are concerned about your safety and they know your identifying information.

## Notes
If you are in immediate danger or need emergency medical help, call **911**.
