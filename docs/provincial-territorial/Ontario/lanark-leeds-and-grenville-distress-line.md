## About
The Distress Centre of Lanark, Leeds and Grenville is a **free, confidential, anonymous** telephone support service, answered by trained volunteers who provide non-judgmental listening support and crisis intervention.

## Coverage
- **Lanark County**
- **Leeds and Grenville**
- (Residents of Lanark, Leeds and Grenville)

## Who it’s for
- Anyone in Lanark, Leeds and Grenville who wants someone to talk to
- People experiencing distress, crisis, or thoughts of suicide
- People feeling lonely or isolated

## Contact
- **Call:** **1-800-465-4442**

## Hours
- **Daily:** **5:00 pm – 12:00 am (midnight)**  
- **365 days/year**

## Languages
Not specified on the service’s official pages.

## What to expect when you call or text
- You’ll be connected with a volunteer who will listen and support you without judgment.
- Volunteers use an active listening approach (they don’t give advice, but help you explore feelings and options).

## Privacy and safety approach
- The service describes itself as **confidential and anonymous**.
