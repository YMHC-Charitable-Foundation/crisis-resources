## About
Reach Out 24/7 is a **free, confidential** mental health and addiction support line offering **call, text, and web chat** support **24/7**.

## Coverage
- **Ontario:** London, **Middlesex, Oxford, and Elgin** counties

## Who it’s for
- Anyone in the coverage area who needs mental health or addiction support for themselves, or someone they care about
- People who are in crisis, overwhelmed, or unsure where to start

## Contact
- **Call:** 519-433-2023
- **Toll-free:** 1-866-933-2023
- **Text:** 519-433-2023
- **Web chat:** https://reachout247.ca/get-help/web-chat/

## Hours
- **24/7/365**

## Languages
- **English and French** (you can choose when you connect)

## What to expect when you call or text
- You may hear a brief introduction message first.
- You’ll be connected with a trained mental health professional, and can choose **English or French**.
- If call volume is high, you may be offered the option to leave a message for a callback.

## Privacy and safety approach
- Support is **confidential**.
- Staff follow confidentiality guidelines, including **duty-to-report** situations when someone is at risk of harm.
