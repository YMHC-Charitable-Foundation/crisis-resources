## About
CMHA Edmonton’s Distress Line provides **free, confidential** support for people who are in crisis or feeling overwhelmed, including **crisis intervention, suicide prevention, emotional support, and referrals**.

## Coverage
Primarily the **Edmonton region**.

## Who it’s for
- Anyone in the Edmonton region experiencing distress or crisis
- People supporting someone else in crisis

## Contact
- **Call:** 780-482-4357 (780-482-HELP)

## Hours
- **24/7/365**

## What to expect when you call
A trained responder will listen, help you explore options, and connect you with local resources and referrals.

## Notes
- CMHA Edmonton materials also list a **24-hour Rural Distress Line (toll-free): 1-800-232-7288** (if you can’t reach the Edmonton number or you’re outside the region).
