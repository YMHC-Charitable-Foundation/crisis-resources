## About
Counselling Alberta offers **accessible counselling** with **no waitlist**, including online counselling available across Alberta and in-person counselling through partner locations in multiple communities.

## Coverage
- **Online counselling:** available anywhere in Alberta  
- **In-person counselling:** available through partner locations in several Alberta communities (see website for current locations)

## Who it’s for
- Individuals, couples, families, children, and youth in Alberta seeking counselling support

## Contact
- **Register online:** https://www.counsellingalberta.com/contact
- **Register by phone:** 833-827-4230
- **Email:** contactus@calgarycounselling.com
- **Website:** https://www.counsellingalberta.com/

## Hours
- **Phone registration:** Monday–Friday, 9:00 a.m.–4:00 p.m.
- **Online registration:** 24/7

## Languages
Some counsellors offer services in multiple languages (availability varies).

## Privacy and safety approach
If you receive in-person counselling outside the Calgary area, your sessions may be provided by a partner agency; Counselling Alberta notes that relevant client information may be shared securely with the service-delivery agency in line with applicable privacy requirements.
