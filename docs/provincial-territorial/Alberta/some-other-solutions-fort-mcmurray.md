## About
**Some Other Solutions (SOS)** provides mental health and wellness supports in the Fort McMurray region, including a **confidential 24/7 crisis line**.

## Coverage
- **Fort McMurray / Wood Buffalo region**
- Serves **Northern Alberta** (per SOS)

## Who it’s for
- People who need immediate crisis support (including emotional support and crisis intervention)
- People looking for counselling and wellness supports in the community
