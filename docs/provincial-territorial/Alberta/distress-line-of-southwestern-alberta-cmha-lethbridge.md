## About
The Distress Line of Southwestern Alberta (CMHA Lethbridge) provides **24/7 telephone support** for people who are in distress and need support, intervention, and/or information and referrals.

## Coverage
**Southwestern Alberta.**

## Who it’s for
- Anyone in Southwestern Alberta who needs distress or crisis support
