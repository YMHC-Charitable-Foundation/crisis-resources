**Location:** Alberta, Canada  
**Resource type:** Health advice & system navigation (nurse line)  
**Hours:** 24/7  

## Contact

- **Call:** 811
- **Website:** https://www.albertahealthservices.ca/info/page12630.aspx

## About

Registered nurse advice and health information; guidance on where to go for care.

## Important

- If you or someone else is in immediate danger, call **911** (or go to the nearest emergency department).
- If you can’t get through, try another local line, or dial **211** where available for service navigation.
