## About
Alberta’s ONE LiNE for Sexual Violence is a **toll-free** talk, text, and chat service that provides **emotional support, information, and referrals** to anyone who has experienced or been impacted by sexual violence.

## Coverage
**Alberta-wide.**

## Who it’s for
- Anyone in Alberta impacted by sexual violence (survivors, support people, and professionals)

## Contact
- **Call or text (toll-free):** 1-866-403-8000
- **Online chat:** Use the “Need Support?” / “Get Support Now” chat box on the website
- **Website:** https://aasas.ca/get-support/

## Hours
- **Daily:** 9:00 a.m.–9:00 p.m. (7 days/week)

## Languages
- **Interpretation services:** available in **200+ languages** (ask when you connect)

## What to expect when you call or text
A responder will listen without judgment, offer support in the moment, and can connect you to the nearest sexual assault centre for more specialized services.

## Privacy and safety approach
- ONE LiNE is **confidential** and does **not** share information without your written permission, **except** in specific safety/legal situations (e.g., risk of suicide, risk of harm to others, child abuse/risk of abuse, or where required by law).

## Notes
- If chat isn’t responding, it may be a technical/connectivity issue—try again or call/text 1-866-403-8000.
