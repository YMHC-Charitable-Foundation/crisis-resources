**Location:** Alberta, Canada  
**Resource type:** Sexual violence support line  
**Hours:** 9:00 a.m.–9:00 p.m., 7 days/week (check for updates)  

## Contact

- **Call:** +1-866-403-8000
- **Text:** +1-866-403-8000
- **Website:** https://aasas.ca/get-support/

## About

Emotional support, information and referrals for anyone impacted by sexual violence in Alberta.

## Important

- If you or someone else is in immediate danger, call **911** (or go to the nearest emergency department).
- If you can’t get through, try another local line, or dial **211** where available for service navigation.
