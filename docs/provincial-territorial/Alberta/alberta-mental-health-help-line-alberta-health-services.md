**Location:** Alberta, Canada  
**Resource type:** Mental health & addiction support, information & referrals  
**Hours:** 24/7  

## Contact

- **Call (toll-free):** +1-877-303-2642
- **Website:** https://www.albertahealthservices.ca/findhealth/Service.aspx?id=6810&serviceAtFacilityID=1047134

## About

Confidential support, information, and referrals. Staffed by mental health professionals.

## Important

- If you or someone else is in immediate danger, call **911** (or go to the nearest emergency department).
- If you can’t get through, try another local line, or dial **211** where available for service navigation.
