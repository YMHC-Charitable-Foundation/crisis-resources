## About
The Alberta Addiction Helpline is a confidential service providing support, information, and referrals for Albertans experiencing addiction concerns, including problem gambling. The line is staffed by a multidisciplinary team of nurses, psychiatric nurses, social workers, occupational therapists, and psychologists.

## Coverage
**Alberta-wide** (toll-free within Alberta).

## Who it's for
- Anyone in Alberta experiencing addiction concerns or problem gambling
- People seeking information about addiction and mental health topics
- Those looking for connections to local addiction and mental health services

## Contact
- **Call:** 1-866-332-2322 (toll-free within Alberta)
- **Alternative:** Dial **811** and follow the prompts to reach the Addiction and Mental Health team
- **Website:** https://www.albertahealthservices.ca/findhealth/service.aspx?Id=1008399

## Hours
- **24/7/365**

## Languages
- English (primary)
- For interpreter support in other languages, ask when you connect

## What to expect when you call
When you contact the helpline, you can expect:
- A confidential conversation with a trained mental health professional
- Crisis support if needed
- Mental health or addiction screening and assessment over the phone
- General information about addiction or mental health topics
- Information about local service options and how to access them
- Strategies and support for emotional well-being

## Cost
- Free to call from within Alberta (toll-free)

## Notes
- The helpline also handles mental health concerns in addition to addiction
- You can also call **811** (Health Link) and request the Addiction and Mental Health helpline
- The service provides rapid access counselling and professional assessment
