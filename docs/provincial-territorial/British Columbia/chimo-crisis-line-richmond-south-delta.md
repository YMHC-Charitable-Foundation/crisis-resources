## About
CHIMO’s Crisis Line provides free, confidential, non-judgmental emotional support for people in **Richmond** and **South Delta**. Support is available by phone, with live chat available through CHIMO’s website.

## Coverage
- **Richmond and South Delta, BC**

## Who it’s for
- People in Richmond or South Delta who are in distress, in crisis, or just need someone to talk to

## Contact

### Crisis Lines (crisis intervention and referral)
- **1-800-SUICIDE:** **1-800-784-2433** — available **24 hours a day, 7 days a week**
- **310-Mental Health Support:** **310-6789** *(do not add 604, 778 or 250 before the number)* — available **24 hours a day, 7 days a week**
- **Chimo Crisis Line (direct):** **604-279-7070** — available **8:00 a.m. to midnight, 7 days a week**
- **Website:** https://chimoservices.com/ (look for "Crisis Line and Live Chat")

## Hours
- **1-800-SUICIDE & 310-Mental Health:** **24/7**
- **Chimo Crisis Line (604-279-7070):** **8:00 a.m.–12:00 a.m. (midnight), 7 days/week**
