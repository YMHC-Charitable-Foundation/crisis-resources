## About
The Crisis Line Association of BC (CLABC) is a provincial association of member crisis lines that maintains province-wide crisis line networks and a directory of local crisis lines in B.C.

## Coverage
- **British Columbia (province-wide)**

## Who it’s for
- Anyone in B.C. who needs:
  - Suicide-related crisis support (1800SUICIDE)
  - Emotional support and mental health resources (310Mental Health Support)

## Contact
- **1800SUICIDE:** 1-800-784-2433
- **310Mental Health Support:** 310-6789 (no area code needed)
- **Local crisis line directory:** CLABC “Map / Crisis Lines”

## Hours
- **1800SUICIDE:** **24/7/365**
- **310Mental Health Support:** **24/7/365**

## What to expect when you call or text
- **1800SUICIDE:** focuses on suicide-related calls, providing suicide assessment/intervention and support, and connecting to resources (and emergency services when needed).
- **310Mental Health Support:** emotional support plus information and resources related to mental health.

## Privacy and safety approach
CLABC describes CARE principles for the province-wide lines, including:
- **Confidential:** calls are kept confidential (in accordance with provincial privacy legislation)
- **Accessible:** toll-free, non-judgemental support is available **24/7/365**
