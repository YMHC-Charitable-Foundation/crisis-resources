## About
The **Crisis Centre for Northern BC** (Crisis Prevention, Intervention & Information Centre for Northern BC) provides **24/7** crisis phone support and youth peer support services for people in Northern British Columbia.

## Coverage
- **Northern British Columbia**

## Who it’s for
- Anyone in **Northern BC** who is overwhelmed, distressed, or needs someone to talk to
- **Youth** looking for peer support (youth-to-youth)

## Contact
- **24-hour Crisis Line:** **1-888-562-1214**
- **24-hour Crisis Line (local):** **250-563-1214**
- **Youth Support Line (call 24/7):** **1-888-564-8336** or **250-564-8336**
- **Youth Text (daily 4:00pm–10:00pm):** **250-564-8336**
- **Youth Support Chat (daily 4:00pm–10:00pm):** Available via https://crisis-centre.ca/ (follow “Youth Support Chat”)
- **Website:** https://crisis-centre.ca/

## Hours
- **Crisis Line:** **24/7/365**
- **Youth Support Line (calls):** **24/7**
- **Youth text and youth chat:** **4:00pm–10:00pm daily**

## What to expect when you call or text
- The crisis line offers confidential, non-judgmental support.
- The youth line is a peer support service operated by trained youth.
