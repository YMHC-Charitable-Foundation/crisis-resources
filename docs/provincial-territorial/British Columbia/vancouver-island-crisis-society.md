## About
Vancouver Island Crisis Society (VICS) provides **short-term, non-judgemental** crisis support, crisis intervention, information, and resource connections through the Vancouver Island Crisis Line, plus online chat and SMS text options.

## Coverage
VICS serves:
- **Vancouver Island**
- The **islands of the Georgia Strait**
- Mainland communities **between Powell River and Rivers Inlet**

## Who it’s for
- People of **all ages** who need crisis support, emotional support, and/or local resource information.

## Contact
- **Crisis Line (24-hour):** 1-888-494-3888
- **Crisis Chat (online):** via VICS website home page
- **Crisis Text (SMS):** 250-800-3806

## Hours
- **Crisis Line:** 24/7
- **Crisis Chat:** daily **6:00 pm – 10:00 pm (Pacific Time)**
- **Crisis Text:** daily **6:00 pm – 10:00 pm (Pacific Time)**

## What to expect when you call or text
- **Crisis Line:** short-term, non-judgemental support and help connecting to resources.
- **Crisis Chat / Text:** confidential emotional support and resource information. You may be asked for **age and gender** (for statistics and to help with appropriate resources). Service workers may end a chat/text if abusive language is used.
- If you’re suicidal or in distress **outside chat/text hours**, VICS directs you to call **1-888-494-3888** (24/7), or **911** for immediate medical/emergency danger.

## Privacy and safety approach
- Chat and text conversations are described as **confidential**; VICS notes that **no video communication** is used in Crisis Chat.
