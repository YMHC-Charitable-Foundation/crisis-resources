## About
**HelpStartsHere.gov.bc.ca** is a BC Ministry of Health website with a searchable directory (2,600+ listings) of mental health and substance use services in British Columbia, plus plain-language articles to help you navigate care.

## Coverage
- **British Columbia**

## Who it’s for
- People in **BC** looking for mental health and/or substance use supports
- People supporting a friend or family member

## Contact
- **Website (search services and information):** https://helpstartshere.gov.bc.ca/

## Notes
HelpStartsHere also lists “get help now” phone lines, including:
- **Crisis Line (suicidal thoughts / distress):** **1-800-SUICIDE (1-800-784-2433)** or **9-8-8**
- **310 Mental Health Support:** **310-6789** (no area code)
- **Opioid Treatment Access Line:** **1-833-804-8111**
- **KUU-US Crisis Response Service (Indigenous):** **1-800-588-8717**
