## About
The Fraser Health Crisis Line provides **immediate, free, confidential** emotional support, crisis intervention, and community resource information by phone.

## Coverage
- Primarily serves the **Fraser Health region** (British Columbia).
- As part of the **BC Crisis Line Network**, it also answers some calls made through provincial lines like **1-800-SUICIDE** and **310-6789**.

## Who it’s for
- People of **all ages** who need emotional support, crisis intervention, and/or help finding local services.

## Contact
- **Call (local):** 604-951-8855
- **Call (toll-free):** 1-877-820-7444

## Hours
- **24/7/365**

## What to expect when you call or text
When you call, you may be:
- Invited to talk about what’s happening and how you’re feeling
- Supported to explore options and coping steps
- Offered information about other community services that could help

## Privacy and safety approach
- Support is described as **free and confidential**.
- For training and quality, **calls may be monitored and recorded**.
