## About
The Crisis Response Centre (CRC) is a **24/7 walk-in** mental health crisis service for **Winnipeg adults**, with crisis assessment, short-term treatment/support, and connection to follow-up resources.

## Coverage
**Winnipeg**.

## Who it’s for
- **Adults (18+) in Winnipeg** experiencing a mental health crisis (including suicidal thoughts/behaviour or severe distress)
- People needing urgent mental health assessment and crisis intervention (and their families/supports)

## Contact
- **Call (Crisis Service / Mobile Crisis):** 204-940-1781
- **Walk-in:** 817 Bannatyne Ave, Winnipeg

## Hours
- **24/7** (walk-ins welcome)

## Notes
- If you need urgent **physical medical care**, are arriving by **ambulance**, or are under the **Mental Health Act**, you may be directed to an Emergency Department.
