## About
The Crisis Stabilization Unit (CSU) provides short-term, community-based supportive care and treatment for Winnipeg adults in psychiatric or psychosocial crisis who may be at risk of hospitalization.

## Coverage
**Winnipeg**.

## Who it’s for
- **Adults in Winnipeg** experiencing a mental health or psychosocial crisis  
- Referrals can come from the individual in crisis and from mental health-care professionals (including crisis, community, and hospital services)

## Contact
- **Call:** 204-940-3633 (press **2**)

## Hours
- **24/7**

## What to expect
When you arrive, a multi-disciplinary team works with you to develop a plan to help manage and resolve the crisis. Services can include crisis intervention, assessment, supportive care, medication management, groups, and referrals.
