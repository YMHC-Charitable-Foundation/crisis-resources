## About
In the Northern Health Region, youth mental health crisis support is available through Hope North’s youth services intake and Mobile Crisis Team, including crisis assessment and support for youth **17 and under**.

## Coverage
Thompson and Northern Health Region communities (Northern Manitoba).

## Who it’s for
- Youth **17 and under**
- Parents/guardians, caregivers, and agencies seeking help for a youth in crisis

## Contact
- **Call (Youth Services Intake / Mobile Crisis):** 1-866-242-1571  
- **Local (Mobile Crisis):** 204-778-1472

## Hours
- Mobile Crisis Team: **12:00 noon – 12:00 midnight (daily)**, plus **8:30 a.m. – 4:30 p.m. (Mon–Fri)** for triage/follow-up

## Notes
- Hope North’s facility in Thompson was reported as temporarily closed for repairs in **September 2025**, but **Mobile Crisis Services and Youth Mental Health remain available by phone**.
