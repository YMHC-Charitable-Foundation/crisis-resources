## About
Mental Wellness and Substance Use Services (Government of Yukon) provides counselling and substance use supports, including withdrawal management services, primarily through the **Sarah Steele Building** in Whitehorse.

## Coverage
**Yukon (territory-wide)** — call the main line or toll-free number to connect to services.

## Who it’s for
People in Yukon seeking support for:
- mental wellness concerns (including counselling supports); and/or
- substance use issues (including withdrawal management services).

## Contact
- **Call (Whitehorse):** 867-456-3838
- **Toll-free (Yukon):** 1-866-456-3838
- **After-hours withdrawal management:** 867-667-8473 (after 4:30 pm or weekends)
- **Website:** https://yukon.ca/en/places/mental-wellness-and-substance-use-services

## Hours
- **Monday to Friday:** 8:00 am to 4:30 pm (main line)
- **After-hours (withdrawal management only):** 867-667-8473

## Languages
Not stated on the service page — ask when you call.

## What to expect when you call
You can call during business hours to:
- book an intake or learn about services; and
- for adult counselling services in Whitehorse, you may be offered a **rapid access counselling** appointment (the site notes an aim of connecting people within **72 hours** of the first call).

## Notes
- **Location (Whitehorse):** Sarah Steele Building, 609 Steele Street.
