**Location:** Nova Scotia, Canada  
**Resource type:** Crisis line  
**Hours:** 24/7  

## Contact

- **Call:** 1-888-429-8167
- **Website:** https://mha.nshealth.ca/en/services/provincial-mental-health-and-addictions-crisis-line

## About

24/7 crisis line support for suicidal thoughts, self-harm, and mental health/addictions crises (see NS Health site).

## Important

- If you or someone else is in immediate danger, call **911** (or go to the nearest emergency department).
- If you can’t get through, try another local line, or dial **211** where available for service navigation.
