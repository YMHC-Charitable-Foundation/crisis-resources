# YMHC Crisis Resources Directory

Welcome to the Youth Mental Health Canada (YMHC) Crisis Resources Directory. This directory provides essential, accessible, and meaningful resources to support students, families, educators, and mental health professionals.

## Explore Resources

*   **[Canada Nationwide Resources](/canada-nationwide)**  
    Access crisis lines and support services available across Canada.

*   **[Provincial & Territorial Resources](/provincial-territorial)**  
    Find specific support services by province or territory.

## About YMHC

YMHC is a not-for-profit, charitable community-based organization focused on mental wellness, resilience, strength, and hope. We empower young people and those who support them with educational resources, workshops, and training.

For more information, visit [ymhc.ngo](https://ymhc.ngo).
