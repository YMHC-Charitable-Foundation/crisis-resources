## About
Trans Lifeline is a **peer support hotline run by trans people for trans and questioning peers**. You do not need to be “in crisis” to call.

## Who it’s for
- Trans and questioning people seeking peer support

## Contact
- **Canada hotline:** 1-877-330-6366
- (Trans Lifeline also lists a separate U.S. hotline on their site.)

## Hours
Trans Lifeline lists set operating hours (not 24/7):
- **Monday to Friday**
  - 10 AM – 6 PM Pacific
  - 1 PM – 9 PM Eastern

## Privacy and safety approach (high-level)
Trans Lifeline states it offers:
- Full anonymity and confidentiality
- A policy against “nonconsensual active rescue” (calling emergency services without explicit request)

## Notes
- Trans Lifeline publishes scheduled hotline closures (see the hotline page for the latest).
