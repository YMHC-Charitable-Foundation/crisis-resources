title: "Wellness Together Canada (Status update)"
---

## Status (important)
The Government of Canada announced that the **Wellness Together Canada** portal would transition away and that it would no longer be accessible as of **April 3, 2024**.

## What to use instead (official federal guidance)
- Government of Canada mental health “get help” page (includes crisis and support options, including 9-8-8):  
  https://www.canada.ca/en/public-health/services/mental-health-services/mental-health-get-help.html

## Primary source
- Government of Canada notice about the Wellness Together Canada portal transition:  
  https://www.canada.ca/en/health-canada/services/mental-health/wellness-together-canada-portal-shut-down.html
