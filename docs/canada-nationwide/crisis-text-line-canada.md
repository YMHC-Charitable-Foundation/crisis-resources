## About
In Canada, crisis texting support is delivered through **Kids Help Phone’s texting service** (often described as a crisis text line).

## Who it’s for
- People in Canada who want **text-based crisis support**, available across Canada

## Contact
- **Text (24/7):** Text **CONNECT** to **686868**
- **Web message (24/7):** Message from a web browser via the Kids Help Phone texting service page

## Hours
- **24/7**

## Languages
- Available in **English and French** (as stated by Kids Help Phone’s texting service page)

## Cost
- Free

## Sources
- Kids Help Phone texting service: https://kidshelpphone.ca/need-help-now-text-us
- Crisis Text Line “Get help” section listing Canada text access: https://www.crisistextline.org/
