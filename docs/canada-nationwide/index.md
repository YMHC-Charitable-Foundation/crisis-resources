This folder contains one markdown file per Canada-wide (or multi-province) crisis/helpline resource, compiled from primary sources.

## Files included
- `9-8-8-suicide-crisis-helpline.md` — Canada’s national suicide crisis helpline (call/text 9-8-8, 24/7).
- `kids-help-phone.md` — Kids Help Phone (call/text support for ages 5–29).
- `crisis-text-line-canada.md` — Crisis texting support in Canada (via Kids Help Phone texting service).
- `hope-for-wellness-helpline.md` — First Nations, Inuit, and Métis Hope for Wellness helpline.
- `national-indian-residential-school-crisis-line.md` — Residential School Crisis Line.
- `mmiwg-crisis-line.md` — Missing and Murdered Indigenous Women and Girls support line.
- `trans-lifeline-canada.md` — Trans Lifeline Canada hotline (limited hours).
- `211-canada.md` — 211 information and referral service.
- `good2talk.md` — Post-secondary support (Ontario and Nova Scotia only; not Canada-wide).
- `wellness-together-canada.md` — Status update: portal transitioned away April 3, 2024.
- `naseeha-mental-health-helpline.md` — Naseeha 24/7 helpline.
- `black-youth-helpline.md` — Black Youth Helpline contact summary (from PDF material).

## Notes on deduplication
- “Talk Suicide Canada” is treated as legacy branding/older materials related to suicide crisis support; the current national three-digit service is **9-8-8**.
