## About
211 is a free, confidential service that helps connect people to **government and community-based, non-clinical health and social services**.

## Who it’s for
- Anyone in Canada looking for navigation to local/community services (including mental health and social supports)

## Contact
- **Dial:** 2-1-1 (where available)
- Access is also offered via **chat, text, and web** (varies by region)

## Hours and languages
- The national 211 site describes access **24 hours a day**
- Available in **150+ languages** (language access varies by region)
