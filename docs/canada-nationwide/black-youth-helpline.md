## About
[Black Youth Helpline (BYH)](https://www.blackyouth.ca/) provides culturally focused support and referral help for **Black youth, families, and communities**.

## Coverage
BYH describes itself as operating **Canada-wide**.

## Who it’s for
- Black youth and young adults
- Parents/caregivers and family members seeking support or referrals
- People looking for culturally relevant mental health, crisis, or community supports

## Contact
- **Toll-free:** **1-833-294-8650** (1-833-BYH-8650)
- **Phone (GTA):** **416-285-9944** or **416-739-8989**
- **Email:** **nationalhelpline@bellnet.ca**
- **Website:** https://www.blackyouth.ca/

## Hours
BYH lists its helpline as available **daily, 9 a.m. – 10 p.m.** (check the website for updates and holiday changes).

## Languages
- BYH does not consistently publish a languages list on public-facing pages; ask when you connect.

## What to expect when you call
BYH materials describe:
- A supportive, culturally relevant conversation
- Help navigating services and referrals, including for mental health and crisis supports
