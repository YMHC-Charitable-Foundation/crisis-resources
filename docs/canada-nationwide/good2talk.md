## About
[Good2Talk](https://good2talk.ca/) provides **free, confidential mental health support** for post-secondary students in **Ontario and Nova Scotia**.

## Coverage
- **Ontario** (Good2Talk Ontario)
- **Nova Scotia** (Good2Talk Nova Scotia)

Eligibility is based on being a **current post-secondary student** in the supported province.

## Who it’s for
- Post-secondary students who want to talk about mental health, stress, anxiety, relationships, discrimination, substance use, or anything else affecting their well-being
- Students who prefer phone, text, or online messaging support

## Hours
- **24/7** (phone and text/messaging, as listed on the Ontario and Nova Scotia service pages)

## Languages
- Ontario’s phone service is available in **100+ languages** with the help of a professionally trained interpreter (see the “100+ languages” page for details).

## What to expect when you call or text
- **Calling:** You can speak with a **professional counsellor**.
- **Texting / online messaging:** You’re connected with a **trained, volunteer crisis responder**. You’ll receive a few automated messages first, including information about the service and privacy policy. You can end a conversation by texting **STOP**.

## Privacy and safety approach
Good2Talk describes conversations as **private/confidential**, and explains that information is not shared outside of the service except in limited safety situations when they have identifying information (see their privacy policy references on-site).
