## About This Website

This directory provides essential, accessible, and meaningful resources to support students, families, educators, and mental health professionals.

## Disclaimers

This directory is not a substitute for professional mental health services. If you are in crisis, please contact emergency services or a mental health professional.

## Accuracy of Information

The information provided in this directory is based on publicly available information and is not exhaustive. We make every effort to ensure the accuracy of the information provided, but we cannot guarantee its accuracy. We are not responsible for any errors or omissions in the information provided.
