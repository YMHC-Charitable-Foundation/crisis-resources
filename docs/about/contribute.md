## Your Contributions Make a Difference

This Crisis Resources Directory exists to help people find the support they need when they need it most. **You can help us make sure that happens.**

Every day, young people, families, educators, and professionals across Canada turn to this directory looking for crisis support. When the information here is accurate and up-to-date, we're doing our part to connect people with help. But we can't do this alone—we need your help to keep this resource as reliable as possible.

### 📋 Help Us Fact-Check
Have you recently contacted one of the resources listed here? Let us know if:
- Contact information (phone numbers, websites, emails) is correct
- Service hours have changed
- A service has expanded to new areas or added new programs
- Eligibility criteria or age ranges have been updated

### 🔄 Update Outdated Information
Organizations change, and so do their services. If you notice:
- A resource is no longer available
- A phone number no longer works
- A website has moved or been redesigned
- Service descriptions that don't match what's currently offered

### ➕ Suggest New Resources
Know of a crisis service that should be included? We want to hear about it! Tell us about:
- New crisis lines or support services
- Culturally-specific resources
- Services for underserved communities
- Regional or local crisis programs

## Getting in Touch

We read every contribution that comes our way. Please send your updates, corrections, or suggestions to:

**ymhc-crisis-resources-database@ymhc.ngo**

When you reach out, it's helpful (but not required) to include:
- The name or URL of the resource you're referring to
- The specific information that needs updating
- The correct or current information (if you have it)
- Any additional context that might be useful

## Thank You

Every correction, update, and suggestion helps ensure that someone in crisis can find the help they're looking for. Whether you're a mental health professional, a community member, someone who's used these services, or simply someone who cares—**thank you for taking the time to help keep this directory accurate.**
